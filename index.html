Our Popular Tours Distribution

Wanderwave sounds like an amazing travel company, offering tours to some of the most popular tourist destinations in the world! Here are some of your most popular tours, based on current trends and traveler preferences:

  • Cox's Bazar Bangladesh: This stunning 120-kilometer stretch of golden sand is a haven for beach lovers. Relax on the shore, take a dip in the turquoise waters, or try some exciting water sports like surfing and parasailing.
  • St. Martin's Island, Bangladesh This tiny island off the coast of Cox's Bazar is a secluded paradise with pristine beaches, coral reefs, and lush greenery. Go snorkeling or diving, explore the island on foot or by bike, or simply relax in a hammock under the palm trees.
  • MaldivesThis island nation in the Indian Ocean is famous for its crystal-clear waters, luxurious resorts, and world-class diving. Spend your days swimming with manta rays and sea turtles, sunbathing on private beaches, or indulging in spa treatments.
  • Kashmir, IndiaThis breathtaking region is known for its stunning mountain scenery, verdant valleys, and crystal-clear lakes. Trek through the Himalayas, go whitewater rafting on the Indus River, or visit the Amarnath Cave, a Hindu pilgrimage site.
  • Himachal Pradesh, India:This mountainous state in northern India is a trekker's paradise, with trails ranging from easy to challenging. Hike through pine forests, apple orchards, and rhododendron meadows, or climb to the top of Rohtang Pass for breathtaking views.
